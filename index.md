## Selected Work

**Deployment Risk Assessment (MCP Server)**  
An analyzer that maps failure modes across robot types and environments by searching domain literature. Built to help teams identify blind spots before deploying: what happens when your warehouse AMR encounters unexpected obstacles, or your manipulation system faces distribution drift it wasn't tested for.

**ConceptOps**  
Infrastructure for temporal abstraction and trajectory evaluation in robotics. Designed to stress-test embodied systems against real deployment scenarios rather than static benchmarks. The thesis: most AI failures happen in the gap between "works in testing" and "works in production."

**Ego2Robot**  
Transformed egocentric human video into robot-consumable training episodes. Built the full pipeline: video preprocessing, action segmentation, and dataset generation for embodied learning systems.

**IVF Outcome Predictor**  
Neural network trained on synthetic patient data to model probabilistic outcomes with uncertainty quantification. End-to-end build: data generation, architecture design, training, and evaluation.

**Vibejam**  
Built a personalized chatbot from scratch to understand modern architectures at the implementation level. Implemented transformer decoder (nanoGPT-style), Mixture-of-Experts, and post-transformer experiments.
